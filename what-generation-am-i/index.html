Why generation dates sometimes differ

Generation labels describe broad social groups, not exact scientific categories. Researchers and publishers may place a boundary one or two years earlier or later. This calculator uses widely cited ranges: Baby Boomers 1946–1964, Gen X 1965–1980, Millennials 1981–1996 and Gen Z 1997–2012.

People born close to a boundary may identify with characteristics of both neighboring generations. These informal overlap groups are sometimes called “Xennials” or “Zillennials.”
