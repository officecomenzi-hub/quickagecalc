Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026  ·  QuickCalcs / QuickAgeCalc

Welcome to QuickAgeCalc ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by QuickCalcs. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit quickagecalc.com.

1. Calculator Information

QuickAgeCalc is a free, client-side tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit dates, ages, or other information you enter into our calculators. Calculator inputs and results are processed locally in your browser.

2. Analytics Information

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors find and use the site. Google Analytics may process information such as:

  • Pages viewed, approximate visit duration, and navigation events
  • Browser, device type, and operating system
  • Referring website or campaign
  • Approximate geographic location, such as country or city
  • Internet Protocol address, which Google Analytics is designed not to log or store in full

This analytics information is used to measure site performance, improve calculators and content, and understand aggregate traffic patterns. It is separate from the information entered into a calculator.

For more information about how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy. Google also provides a Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to distinguish visits and measure usage. You can restrict or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking analytics cookies does not prevent you from using the calculators.

4. Advertising

QuickAgeCalc does not currently display active third-party advertising. If advertising services such as Google AdSense are enabled in the future, this policy will be updated before or when those services become active.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

QuickAgeCalc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through our calculators.

7. Data Security

Our site is served over HTTPS. Because calculator inputs are processed in the browser and are not sent to us, we do not maintain a database containing the dates or results entered into the tools.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws. These may include rights related to access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection concerning personal data processed through analytics services.

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@quickagecalc.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when site features or third-party services change. The latest revision date will always appear at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@quickagecalc.com.
