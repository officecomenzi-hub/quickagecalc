Frequently Asked Questions

How is my exact age calculated?
Your age is calculated by finding the difference between your date of birth and today's date (or a specified date). We account for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you a precise result in years, months, and days.
How many days old am I?
The calculator shows your total age in days above. On average, a 30-year-old person has lived approximately 10,950 days, accounting for leap years.
Can I calculate my age on a specific date?
Yes! Simply fill in the "Age at date" fields with the target date. This is useful for finding your age on a birthday, anniversary, or any future event.
What is the most accurate age calculator?
Our calculator is fully accurate — it accounts for all leap years between your birth date and the target date, and computes exact months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes.
