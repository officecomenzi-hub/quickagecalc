How Old Am I In Minutes?

To find how many minutes old you are, multiply your total days alive by 1,440 (the number of minutes in a day). The result is your total age in minutes since birth.

How to Calculate Your Age in Minutes

The formula is: Total minutes = total days alive × 1,440. One day = 24 hours × 60 minutes = 1,440 minutes. For a 36-year-old with approximately 13,149 days alive: 13,149 × 1,440 = 18,934,560 minutes.

Putting Minutes in Perspective

A 30-year-old has lived approximately 15,778,800 minutes. Of those, roughly 5,209,000 minutes have been spent sleeping (assuming 8 hours per night). That leaves approximately 10,519,800 waking minutes — the time available for everything else in life so far.

The 10 Million Minute Milestone

Your 10,000,000th minute alive happens at approximately age 19 years and 1 month. Your 20,000,000th minute at around age 38 years and 1 month. These milestones are rarely celebrated but are surprisingly meaningful when you encounter them.
