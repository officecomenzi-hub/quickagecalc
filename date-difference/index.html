How to Calculate the Difference Between Two Dates

Calculating the number of days between two dates is useful in many everyday situations — from planning a trip or project deadline to finding out how long ago an event occurred. Our date difference calculator makes it effortless.

Simply select your start date and end date, click calculate, and you'll instantly see the difference expressed in years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Common Uses for a Date Difference Calculator

  • Project planning — Calculate how many working days remain until a deadline
  • Anniversary tracking — Find out exactly how long you've been together
  • Travel planning — Count days until your next trip
  • Legal and financial — Calculate contract durations or loan periods
  • Health and fitness — Track how long you've maintained a habit or routine
  • Historical research — Find the gap between any two historical dates

Does It Account for Leap Years?

Yes — our calculator fully accounts for leap years. A leap year occurs every 4 years (with some exceptions for century years), adding an extra day in February. This means that if your date range spans a leap year, that extra day is correctly included in the total count.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the number of days between two dates?
Enter your start date and end date in the fields above and click "Calculate Date Difference." You'll instantly see the result in days, weeks, months, and years.
Can I calculate dates in the past and future?
Yes — our calculator works with any dates from 1900 to 2100, whether in the past or future. You can calculate how many days ago something happened, or how many days until a future event.
What is the difference between calendar days and business days?
Calendar days include all days of the week (including weekends and holidays). Business days typically exclude weekends and public holidays. Our calculator shows calendar days — for business days, simply subtract weekends from the total.
Is this date calculator free?
Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no limits, no personal data collected. All calculations happen instantly in your browser.
