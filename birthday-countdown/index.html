How Many Days Until My Birthday?

To find out how many days until your birthday, enter your birth date above and our calculator will give you the exact countdown — updated every day. The result includes days, weeks, hours, and the day of the week your birthday falls on this year.

How to Calculate Days Until Your Birthday

The calculation depends on whether your birthday has already passed this year or not:

  • Birthday not yet passed: count the days from today to your birthday this year
  • Birthday already passed: count the days from today to your birthday next year

For example, if your birthday is July 15 and today is May 24, 2026, your birthday is 51 days away. If your birthday was March 10, it already passed — your next birthday is March 10, 2027, which is 290 days away.

What Day of the Week Is My Birthday This Year?

Your birthday falls on a different day of the week each year. Birthdays shift forward by 1 day each year, and by 2 days after a leap year. The calculator above shows you exactly what day of the week your birthday falls on in 2026.

How Many Days Until My Birthday — By Month

Approximate days until upcoming birthdays from late May 2026:

  • June birthday: approximately 7 to 37 days away
  • July birthday: approximately 38 to 68 days away
  • August birthday: approximately 69 to 99 days away
  • September birthday: approximately 100 to 129 days away
  • December birthday: approximately 191 to 221 days away

Your Zodiac Sign and Birthday

Your Zodiac sign is determined by your birthday. The calculator above shows your sign automatically. Birthdays in late May fall under Gemini (May 21 to June 20). Each sign has different date ranges that shift slightly depending on the exact time of year.

The Most Common Birthday Month

September is statistically the most common birth month globally. The most common single birthday in the United States is September 9. The least common birthday is February 29, which only exists in leap years — meaning people born on that date only have a real birthday every 4 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many days until my birthday?
Enter your birth date in the calculator above to find the exact number of days until your next birthday. The result updates daily so you always have the current countdown.
How do I calculate days until my birthday manually?
Count the remaining days in the current month, then add all days in the months between now and your birth month, then add the days in your birth month up to your birth day. Remember to check whether your birthday has already passed this year — if so, count to next year.
How many days until my birthday if I was born in July?
If you were born in July and today is late May 2026, your birthday is approximately 38 to 68 days away depending on your exact birth date. Enter your date above for the precise countdown.
What day of the week is my birthday this year?
Enter your birth date in the calculator above. It will show you exactly what day of the week your birthday falls on in 2026, along with your Zodiac sign.
How many weeks until my birthday?
Divide the number of days until your birthday by 7. For example, if your birthday is 70 days away, it is exactly 10 weeks away. The calculator shows both days and weeks automatically.
What is the most common birthday?
The most common birthday in the United States is September 9. September is also the most common birth month globally. The least common birthday is February 29 (leap day), which only occurs every 4 years.
How many hours until my birthday?
Multiply the days until your birthday by 24. For example, if your birthday is 30 days away, it is approximately 720 hours away. The calculator above shows the exact hours count automatically.
What is a golden birthday?
A golden birthday (also called lucky birthday) is when you turn the age that matches your birth date. For example, turning 15 on the 15th of any month. If you were born on the 25th, your golden birthday is when you turn 25.
