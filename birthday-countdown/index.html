Frequently Asked Questions

How many days until my birthday?
Enter your birth month, day, and year above and click calculate. You'll see the exact number of days, hours, minutes, and seconds until your next birthday.
What if my birthday is today?
If today is your birthday, the calculator will show 0 days and display a special birthday message. Happy Birthday! 🎉
Does the calculator account for leap year birthdays?
Yes — if you were born on February 29th, the calculator automatically handles leap year birthdays correctly, showing the next February 29th or March 1st depending on the year.
