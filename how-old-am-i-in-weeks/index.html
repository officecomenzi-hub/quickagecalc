How Old Am I In Weeks?

To find how many weeks old you are, divide your total days alive by 7. Since each week has exactly 7 days, this gives a precise count of complete weeks you have lived.

How to Calculate Your Age in Weeks

First find your total days alive (years × 365 + leap years + remaining days), then divide by 7 and round down to get complete weeks. The calculator above does this automatically.

Age in Weeks by Age

  • Age 10: approximately 521 weeks
  • Age 20: approximately 1,043 weeks
  • Age 30: approximately 1,565 weeks
  • Age 40: approximately 2,087 weeks
  • Age 50: approximately 2,608 weeks
  • Age 80: approximately 4,173 weeks

The 2,000 Week Milestone

Your 2,000th week alive happens at approximately age 38 years and 5 months. If you live to 80, you will experience approximately 4,173 weeks total — a number that many people find surprisingly small when they think about how precious each week is.

Why Count Age in Weeks?

Weeks are a natural unit of time for most people — work weeks, school weeks, and planning cycles are all weekly. Knowing your age in weeks gives a meaningful perspective on how many "units" of productive time you have lived and how many remain.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many weeks old am I?
Enter your birth date above to find your exact age in weeks. The calculator accounts for all leap years between your birth date and today.
How do I calculate my age in weeks?
Divide your total days alive by 7. For example, a 30-year-old has lived approximately 10,958 days, which equals about 1,565 weeks.
How many weeks old am I if I was born in 1990?
If you were born in 1990, you are approximately 1,870 to 1,885 weeks old in 2026, depending on your exact birth date.
How many weeks in a year?
There are exactly 52 weeks and 1 day in a standard year, and 52 weeks and 2 days in a leap year. This is why your birthday shifts forward by 1 day each year.
How many weeks old am I if I was born in 2000?
If you were born in 2000, you are approximately 1,340 to 1,356 weeks old in 2026, depending on your exact birth date.
