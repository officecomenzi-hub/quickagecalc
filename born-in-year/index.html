How to Use This Page

Simply click on your birth year above to get your exact age in 2026, including a breakdown in years, months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes. Each page also includes a precise calculator where you can enter your exact birth date.

Why Does My Age Vary by 1 Year?

Your age depends on whether your birthday has already passed in 2026. Before your birthday, you are still the younger age. On and after your birthday, you turn the older age. This is why we always show both possibilities — use the calculator for your exact answer.
