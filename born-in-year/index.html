How to Use This Page

Click on your birth year to get your exact age in 2026, including a breakdown in years, months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes. Each page also includes a precise calculator where you can enter your exact birth date.

Why Does My Age Vary by 1 Year?

Your age depends on whether your birthday has already passed in 2026. Before your birthday, you are still the younger age. On and after your birthday, you turn the older age.

What Generation Am I?

Generations are defined by birth year ranges: Baby Boomers (1946-1964), Generation X (1965-1980), Millennials (1981-1996), Generation Z (1997-2012), and Generation Alpha (2013-present). Click your birth year above to see your exact generation and age.
