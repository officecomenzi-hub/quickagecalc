How to Calculate Age Difference

The age difference between two people is simply the number of years, months, and days between their birth dates. Our calculator takes both birthdays, determines who is older, and shows the precise difference in multiple units.

Common Uses

Age difference calculators are popular for couples checking their compatibility, siblings comparing their ages, parents tracking the gap between children, and even historical research comparing birth years of famous figures.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the age difference between two people?
Simply enter both birth dates above and click Calculate. The tool will instantly tell you who is older and the exact difference in years, months, weeks, and days.
Can I use names for each person?
Yes — the name fields are optional but make the result more personal. Enter any name or nickname for each person.
What if both people have the same birthday?
If both birth dates are exactly the same, the calculator will show an age difference of 0 years, 0 months, and 0 days — they are the same age!
