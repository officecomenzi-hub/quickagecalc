How to calculate your age in another year

A quick estimate is the target year minus your birth year. The exact result can be one year lower when the target date falls before your birthday. This calculator compares the full dates, including leap years and the different number of days in each month.

  • Use a past date to answer “How old was I in 2001?”
  • Use a future date to answer “How old will I be in 2030?”
  • Use today’s date to confirm your exact current age.
