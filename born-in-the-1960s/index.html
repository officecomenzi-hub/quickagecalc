Are People Born in the 1960s Boomers or Gen X?

It depends on the exact year. The Baby Boomer generation runs from 1946 to 1964, and Generation X runs from 1965 to 1980. That means the 1960s are split right down the middle: 1960–1964 are Baby Boomers and 1965–1969 are Generation X. Someone born in 1964 is the last of the Boomers, and someone born in 1965 is the first of Gen X.

How Old Are 1960s Babies in 2026?

People born in the 1960s turn between 56 and 66 in 2026. The oldest, born in 1960, reach 66, while the youngest, born in 1969, reach 57. Many in this group are at or near the standard retirement age of 65, so a number of them are planning retirement around now.
