How Old Am I In Months?

To find how many months old you are, multiply your age in years by 12, then add the number of months since your last birthday. This gives your total age in complete months.

How to Calculate Your Age in Months

The formula is: Total months = (years × 12) + months since last birthday. For example, if you are 36 years and 5 months old, your age in months is (36 × 12) + 5 = 437 months.

Age in Months for Babies and Toddlers

For children under 2 years old, age in months is the standard medical measurement. Pediatricians track development in months because growth and milestones change rapidly in the first two years. A 18-month-old is very different developmentally from a 24-month-old, even though both are "about 1-2 years old."

When Do We Stop Counting in Months?

Most parents stop counting age in months around 24 months (2 years), switching to years. However, for medical and developmental tracking, months are often used until age 3 or even 5.
